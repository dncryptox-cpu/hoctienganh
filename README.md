# 🏔️ Self-Study English: Communications in Trekking

An interactive, slide-based English lesson designed for self-study.

## 📚 Topics Covered
- **Unit 1** – Meeting your guide & trail briefing
- **Unit 2** – On the trail: describing conditions & asking for help
- **Unit 3** – Reaching the campsite: reflecting & planning

## 🎯 Learning Goals
After completing this lesson, you can:
- Introduce yourself and communicate with a trekking guide
- Ask for help, describe how you feel, and respond to instructions on the trail
- Talk about your trekking experience and plans for the next day

## ✨ Features
- 42 interactive slides with navigation (← →, keyboard arrows, spacebar)
- AI Text-to-Speech (male & female voices via Web Speech API)
- IPA pronunciation for every sentence and vocabulary item
- A1 → B1 leveled example sentences for each word/chunk
- Dictation + Yes/No + Wh- comprehension drills
- Pronunciation & Shadowing exercises
- Printable PDF layout

## 🚀 How to Use
Open `communications_in_trekking.html` in **Chrome** or **Edge** for the best TTS experience.

> **Tip:** Use Edge with "Natural" voices for the most realistic pronunciation.
